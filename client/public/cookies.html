Cookie Policy

This page explains how Adoptar.io uses cookies.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. They help the site remember your choices and keep you signed in.

Cookies we use

Session cookie – Keeps you signed in for the session. Essential for playing.

Guest name cookie – If you play as a guest we may store a generated name (e.g. rescue123). Essential for guest play.

Preferences – We may store preferences (e.g. music on/off) in local storage. Not sent to the server.

Your choices

You can choose Accept (all cookies) or Essential only. Use Privacy settings to change your choice. See Privacy Policy for more.
